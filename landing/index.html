How To Use

IDetect is Website Extention that can get your Id image, and fill your fields with your details automatically.

So, If you want to add a script to your website and get our amazing functionality- feel free to join us.
How to use

Get a script

Get link to our script file, add it to your site. You will find that your site has a button added.

Installation

In the first click on the button, will open install page. You have been click on every field in your site Respectively the card fields.

Use

Then, every time you will open the button- you will be able to include card image, and all the fields will fill by the card.
