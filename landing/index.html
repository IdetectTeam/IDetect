How To Use?

IDetect is Website Extention that can get your Id image, and fill your fields with your details automatically.

So, If you want to add a script to your website and get our amazing functionality- feel free to join us.


Add a script

Copy this code to require page in your site. You will find that your site has a button added.


Installation

After adding the script, the first time the button is pressed, the installation process begins.
In order to avoid errors later, it is important that you perform it accurately.
You will need to match the existing passport fields with the corresponding fields on your page.
When you click a field you will see a sign so you can keep track of configuration,
If you don't have a parallel field, just click on next button.
At the end of the process, you can see the fields you have selected,
Check it carefully, after it you won't be able to change again!


Use

Then, every time you or your users will open the button it will be able to upload an image or take a picture,
and all the fields in your page will fill automatically.

Why choose us?

Speed

Using the extension will save you valuable time manually entering the fields, and will automatically fill in the fields that you have in the certificate you uploaded. especially great customer services.

Reliability

The image you upload is sent in a secure connection, processed to fill the fields, and returned to you. It is not stored on any server and is used for no other purpose than your needs.
